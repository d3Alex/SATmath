Advance. Sample task 1

A group of friends decided to divide the $800 cost of a trip equally among themselves. When two of the friends decided not to go on the trip, those remaining still divided the $800 cost equally, but each friend's share of the cost increased by $20. How many friends were in the group originally?

  • Let x be the original number of friends. therefore the equally shared cost of the trip for each friend is $800/x.

    After 2 friends drop out, the new cost for each friend become $800/(x-2).

    The increase in cost for each remaining friend is $20, therefore 800/(x-2) - 800/x = 20 or 40/(x-2) - 40/x = 1

    after dividing both sides by 20 and simlifying, we get the quadratic equation 40x - 40(x-2)=(x-2)x or x² - 2x - 80 = 0

    Factoring the equation above (x + 8)(x - 10) = 0 gives two roots x = -8 or x = 10

    Reject x = -8 because we cannot have a negative number for friends, we get the answer: there were 10 friends in the original group.

    thus either ( a , b ) = ( 3 , 5 ) or ( a , b ) = ( 5 , 3 )
