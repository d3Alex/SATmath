Geometry. Sample task 1

"O" is the center of the circle below. What is the value of angle α

  • Let's mark two more angles: β and γ (see the picture)

    γ is an agle that add α to 360°, so

    γ = 360 - α

  • β is an inscribed angle on circle and can be calculated using inscribed angle theorem

    an inscribed angle on circle is half the measure of the central angle that forms the same arc

    β = α 2

  • The following equation is based on formula: sum of interior angles = (n - 2) > 180° where 'n' is the number of sides of a polygon.

    β + γ + 2 * 20 = 360

  • We can plug expressions for β and γ into the equation

    α 2 + ( 360 - α ) + 2 * 20 = 360

    Solving for α gives α = 80°
